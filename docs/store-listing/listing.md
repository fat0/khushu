## Full Description
Khushu is a free, ad-free Islamic prayer app with a clean, simple interface to help you wherever you are in your salat journey.

Whether you're a revert learning to pray, a child being introduced to salat, or a lifelong practitioner checking prayer times while traveling — Khushu is for you.

Features:
- Accurate prayer times for both Sunni and Shia (Ja'fari) fiqhs
- Dual Asr times showing both standard and Hanafi calculations
- Tap any prayer to see rakat breakdown — fard, sunnah, nafilah, witr
- Fiqh-aware labels: Sunnah for Sunni, Nafilah for Ja'fari
- GPS auto-detection with manual city search
- Regional calculation methods auto-selected based on your location
- Timezone-aware clock for any location worldwide
- Light and dark mode following your system theme
- No ads. No tracking. No data collection.

Ja'fari rakat data verified against Ayatollah Sistani's Tawdih al-Masa'il.

One Ummah Serving Allah.
