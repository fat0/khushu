Khushu Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

Khushu is a free, ad-free Islamic prayer times app. We respect your privacy and collect no personal data.

Data We Collect

None. Khushu does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We do not have servers.

Location Data

Khushu requests your device's GPS location to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. Your location coordinates are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never sent to our servers (we have none)
  • Used solely to fetch prayer times from the AlAdhan API

Third-Party Services

Khushu uses the AlAdhan Prayer Times API to calculate prayer times. When fetching prayer times, your GPS coordinates are sent to the AlAdhan API. Please refer to AlAdhan's privacy policy for their data practices.

Advertising and Analytics

Khushu contains no ads and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Data Storage

All app data (your selected fiqh, location, and cached prayer times) is stored locally on your device using Hive, a lightweight local database. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

Khushu does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the changes here with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at github.com/fat0/khushu.
